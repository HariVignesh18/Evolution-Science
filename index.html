The Evolution of Knowledge

Fire

Fire was discovered by early humans, allowing them to cook food, stay warm, and protect themselves.

Steam Engine

The steam engine, developed in the 18th century, powered the Industrial Revolution, transforming transportation and manufacturing processes.

Electricity

Electricity, harnessed for centuries, powers our modern world. From lighting our homes to energizing technology, it's the lifeblood of innovation.

Printing Press

Invented by Johannes Gutenberg, the printing press revolutionized communication by making books accessible and affordable, fostering literacy.

Telecommunication

Telecommunication has connected the world, enabling instant communication across vast distances, shaping our modern society.

Internet

The Internet connects billions of people worldwide. It revolutionized communication, commerce, and access to information, shaping the modern digital age.
